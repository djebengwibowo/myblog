About Me

For more than 25 years, I’ve operated at the intersection of finance, growth, and customer strategy — helping businesses transform complexity into opportunity. From corporate finance to lifecycle management and business development, my career has been driven by one core belief: the best solutions are built around real human needs.

Solution selling, to me, isn’t just a professional skill — it’s a philosophy. It’s about listening deeply, diagnosing accurately, and building solutions that genuinely simplify everyday life.

Today, I’m channeling that same mindset into product innovation. I build web applications that enhance my own daily routines and hobbies — small, practical tools born from curiosity and experimentation. It’s my way of staying hands-on, creative, and future-ready.

If you’re passionate about building meaningful solutions and continuously evolving, we’ll likely have a lot to talk about.
