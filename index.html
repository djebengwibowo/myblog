About Me

For over 25 years, I've worked at the crossroads of finance, growth, and customer strategy—helping businesses turn complexity into opportunity. Whether in corporate finance, business development, or customer lifecycle management, my career has always been guided by a simple principle: the most effective solutions are those designed around real human needs.

To me, solution selling is more than a professional skill—it's a mindset. It's about listening with intent, diagnosing with precision, and crafting answers that genuinely make life easier.

Today, I bring that same approach to product innovation. I build web applications that solve everyday problems—born out of curiosity, a bit of experimentation, and a desire to stay hands-on and creatively engaged. I'm especially fascinated by the potential of AI and machine learning, and I'm actively sharpening my skills in this space to build smarter, more intuitive tools.

Check out my blog on a regular basis—I'll keep adding new tools that, hopefully, can make our lives a little easier and more productive.

If you're driven by a passion for building things that matter—and you believe in continuously evolving along the way—we'll likely have plenty to talk about.
